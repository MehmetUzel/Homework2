Karaman

Population: 156.536

Traffic Code: 70

The province of Karaman is located to the south of the Central Anatolian Region and is between Konya – İçel – Antalya provinces.
The province is a major commerce, culture and art center. Karaman province and its vicinity is known to be inhabited from the
beginning of BC 8000 and the region charms and fascinates the visitors with the touristic beauties as underground cities, caves, religious
centers and also with natural beauties as plateaus and other natural flora and fauna, rich hand crafted art works.
